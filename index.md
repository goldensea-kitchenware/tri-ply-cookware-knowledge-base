## How to Use This Reference

Use the guides to define measurable requirements before requesting quotations, then verify the agreed construction through drawings, samples, inspections, and applicable documentation. A label such as “tri-ply,” “18/10,” or “induction-ready” is a starting point, not a complete specification.
